Life Reminders Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

In short: Life Reminders stores all your data on your device (and optionally in your personal iCloud for Pro users). We don't collect, transmit, or sell your personal information. Voice processing happens entirely on-device.

Data Storage

All reminders, family member profiles, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Your data is not uploaded to any server owned or operated by us.

iCloud Sync (Pro Plan Only)

If you upgrade to Life Reminders Pro, your reminders sync across your iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch using Apple's CloudKit framework. This data is stored in your personal private iCloud database, which only you can access. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

iCloud sync is governed entirely by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy. You can disable iCloud sync at any time by signing out of iCloud or disabling iCloud Drive for Life Reminders in your device settings.

The Free and Ad-Supported plans do not use iCloud sync — data stays local to a single device.

Voice Input

Life Reminders uses Apple's on-device speech recognition (SFSpeechRecognizer) to convert your voice into text. All voice processing happens locally on your device. No audio recordings or transcripts are sent to our servers or any third party. Voice input is available on iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

Notifications

Life Reminders uses Apple's local notification system to send you reminders. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device. No push notification server is involved. Each device (iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch) schedules its own local notifications from your synced reminder data.

Advertising (Ad-Supported Plan)

If you choose the free ad-supported plan on iPhone or iPad, Life Reminders displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect and use certain device information (such as advertising identifiers) to serve relevant ads. This data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's privacy settings (Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking).

The Free plan (without ads), the Pro plan, and the Mac/Apple Watch versions do not display any advertisements and do not share any data with advertising networks.

Analytics & Tracking

Life Reminders does not include any analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, or user tracking mechanisms beyond what is required for ad delivery in the ad-supported plan.

In-App Purchases

Life Reminders Pro is available as a one-time in-app purchase processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to any third party. The only exception is the advertising data described above, which applies only to the iOS ad-supported plan.

Children's Privacy

Life Reminders does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed as a general-purpose reminder tool for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
