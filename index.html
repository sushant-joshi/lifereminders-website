Why Life Reminders?

🎤

Voice-First Input

Say "Mom's passport expires June 2028" — the app understands category, person, and date. On-device on iOS and modern Android (API 31+); on-device-preferred fallback elsewhere.

🕑

Smart Grace Periods

Passport alerts start 1 year out (6-month travel rule). Bills alert 3 days before. Each category knows when to worry.

Auto-Recurrence

Overdue Nagging

Three Views
