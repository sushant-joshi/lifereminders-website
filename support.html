Getting Started

Adding Reminders

  • iPhone iPad Tap the blue mic button (voice) or keyboard button (manual)
  • Mac Click the mic icon in the toolbar (Cmd+R) or + icon (Cmd+N)
  • Apple Watch View and complete reminders created on other devices

Voice example: "Mom's passport expires June 2028" — the app understands category, person, and date automatically.

Three Views

  • Dashboard — reminders grouped by urgency
  • Timeline — reminders grouped by time horizon (week/month/year/5 years/10 years)
  • Calendar — week, month, or year view with bell indicators on dates with reminders

iPhone Swipe left/right on the Reminders tab to switch views.

iPad Mac Use the sidebar to navigate between views.

Completing Reminders

iPhone iPad Swipe right on any reminder to mark it done.

Mac Right-click a reminder → Complete, or press Cmd+Return in detail view.

Apple Watch Tap the reminder → Done button.

Recurring reminders (birthdays, taxes, passport renewals) automatically schedule the next occurrence.

Multi-Device & iCloud Sync

How does sync work?

Life Reminders Pro uses iCloud to sync your reminders across all your Apple devices. Add a reminder on your iPhone, complete it on your Mac, check it on your Watch — all in real-time.

Each device schedules its own local notifications, so you'll get alerts wherever you are.

Do I need Pro for sync?

Yes. iCloud sync is a Pro feature ($4.99 one-time). The Free and Ad-Supported plans work on a single device only.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why am I not getting notifications?

Go to Settings (in app) and check Push Notifications is "Enabled." If not, tap "Enable" to open iOS/macOS Settings.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Settings → Your Plan → "Restore Purchases." Use the same Apple ID as the original purchase. Pro is a Family Sharing-compatible purchase, so family members on the same Apple Family can share it.

Is my data backed up?

Free and Ad-Supported plans: data is stored on-device and included in iCloud/iTunes device backups. Pro plan: data is automatically synced to your private iCloud database and available on all your devices.

How do I remove ads?

Settings → Your Plan → "Upgrade to Pro" ($4.99 one-time). Removes ads, unlocks unlimited reminders, and enables iCloud sync across all devices.

Does voice input need internet?

No. All voice processing is on-device via Apple's SFSpeechRecognizer. Works on iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

Does Apple Watch have voice input?

Not directly within Life Reminders. Use voice input on your iPhone or Mac — the reminder will sync to your Watch automatically (Pro plan).
